George Brown College (GBC) is a public, accredited institution located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, founded in 1967. It offers a wide range 
of programs including diplomas, degrees, and certificates in fields like business, technology, health sciences, community services, and arts and
design. With multiple campuses such as St. James, Waterfront, and Casa Loma, GBC provides both full-time, part-time, and online learning options.
The college has strong ties with industry, giving students opportunities for internships, co-ops, and practical experience through partnerships 
with local employers. Additionally, GBC serves a diverse international student population, offering services to help them adjust to life in Canada. 
The college is accredited by the Ontario Ministry of Colleges and Universities and is a member of Colleges and Institutes Canada (CICan). Along with
academic offerings, GBC provides various student support services, including career counseling and extracurricular activities, fostering a well-rounded
campus experience.
